Biography

Ekoa came together in Kraków at the end of 2019 under the leadership of Paweł Staszewski and the rhythm guitarist, Kamil Kowalczyk, who was the band's first member. Initially, they collaborated with a session drummer until Sergio Barbero joined them in early 2020. This marked a significant step forward for the band's creative process. However, they faced challenges in finding a frontman and bass player. After numerous auditions and brief collaborations with various musicians, Kamil discovered Aurel's solo project, Karthesis, and extended an invitation for him to join Ekoa.

Aurel turned out to be an ideal fit for the band as he possessed the ability to sing and play bass guitar simultaneously, complementing the songs with his melodic style. With Aurel's arrival, the lineup was complete, and the band quickly molded their songs, culminating in the release of their EP, "Chrysalis," in April 2022. The debut CD release propelled Ekoa into the local underground scene, where they garnered interest, recognition, and a growing fan base, as well as catching the attention of promoters and fellow musicians.

Building on their initial success, Ekoa continued to dedicate themselves to creating new material for their highly anticipated first full-length album, slated for release in 2023.
